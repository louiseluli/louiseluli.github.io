About Me

I am a multifaceted, deeply reflective, and intellectually audacious Black Brazilian woman whose work pulses with resistance, tenderness, and transformation.

Originally from Mesquita in the Baixada Fluminense region of Rio de Janeiro, my story is one of relentless transcendence. I bridge the margins and center of global conversations on power, technology, race, and desire.

My academic trajectory—from International Relations to Cultural Studies and now Artificial Intelligence—is revolutionary. At every stage, I reclaim the right to question: Who defines intelligence? Who is protected in cyberspace? Who gets to feel safe, loved, desired, or heard in the digital age?
